About Me

From Dewas, my journey into the creative world began with a passion for Fashion Design, which later evolved into a deep love for Graphic Design. After working in Mumbai’s fast-paced fashion industry and collaborating with renowned stylists and celebrities, I transitioned into the digital space, blending my artistic vision with technology.

The pandemic led me to explore Graphic Design further, and I honed my skills through real-world experience. Now, as I pursue my Master’s in Design, I continue to push creative boundaries, combining aesthetics, storytelling, and technical expertise to bring ideas to life. Every project I take on is an opportunity to innovate, inspire, and create something meaningful.
